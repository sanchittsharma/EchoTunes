Accessibility at Echo Tunes

Welcome to Echo Tunes, where music is for everyone! Here's how we ensure accessibility:

1. **Keyboard Navigation**: Easily navigate the site without a mouse.

2. **Screen Reader Compatibility**: Our site works seamlessly with screen readers.

3. **Alt Text for Images**: All images have descriptive text.

4. **Color Contrast**: Text and buttons are easy to see.

5. **Resizable Text**: Adjust font size to your preference.

6. **Accessible Forms**: Easy-to-use forms with clear labels.

7. **Captioned Content**: Captions provided for audio/video.

8. **Feedback and Support**: Contact us for assistance.
